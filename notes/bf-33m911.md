## Key Finding

The combination `-vv --tb=short` is optimal because:
- `-vv` provides maximum verbosity with character-level diffs
- `--tb=short` gives readable tracebacks with line numbers and assertion details
- Together they show exact location, expected vs actual values, and detailed diffs
