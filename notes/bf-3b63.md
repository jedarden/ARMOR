### Debug Levels Available
- **Minimal:** `RUST_LOG=needle::strand::pluck=debug`
- **Comprehensive:** `RUST_LOG=needle::strand::pluck=trace`
- **Full Context:** `RUST_LOG=needle::strand=debug,needle::strand::pluck=trace`
- **Complete Worker:** `RUST_LOG=needle::strand::pluck=trace,needle::strand=debug,needle::bead_store=debug,needle::worker=debug,needle::dispatch=debug`

## What Gets Logged

With the recommended configuration, you'll see:
1. **Pluck evaluation start** - Configuration values used
2. **Bead store queries** - Filters applied
3. **Candidate counts** - How many beads passed initial filters
4. **Label filtering** - Which beads are excluded and why
5. **Status/assignee filtering** - Beads excluded due to status conflicts
6. **Sorting results** - How candidates are prioritized
7. **Split decisions** - Whether bead splitting is triggered
8. **Final results** - What Pluck selected or why it found nothing
